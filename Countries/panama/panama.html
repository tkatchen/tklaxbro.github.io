Bienvenido a Panama!

  Panama is a country located in Central America, to the southeast of Costa Rica. It is known mostly for the Panama Canal, an arificial waterway that connects the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans. Panama is also home of sjungles where some exoxtic animals live, only found in the Panama jungles. Some positives about Panama are:

  • Very cheap living, you don't have to pay laborers high and you can usually buy food for 75% the cost as it is in America.
  • Accessible health and dental care, it is roughly half the price as it is in America.
  • A nice tropical climite, staying around a constant 88-75 degrees year-round.
  • They have a strong cultural identity and is a great place if you want to learn about a completely different culuture.
