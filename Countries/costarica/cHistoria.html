Spanish Control.

  • On Sep 18, 1502, Spanish Conquistador, Christopher Columbus, landed on the eastern side of Costa Rica
  • After that, in 1542, the first colony, Villa Bruselas was founded.
  • Some struggles included: Lack of gold and silver, few inhabitants, lacked indigenous people for forced labor, and distance from the captial, Guatemala
  • Left to govern iteself by Spain and turned into a democracy.

Independence.

  • Costa Rica got it's independence on Sep. 15, 1821 during the last Mexican War of Independence, even though they never fought in the battle.
  • After Independence two political parties formed: The Imperialists who wanted to join the Mexican Empire The Republicans who wanted full independence. (These guys won)
