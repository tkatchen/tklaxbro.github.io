Attracciónes en Costa Rica!

  Costa Rica is known mostly for it's outside activities, these include waterfalls, hiking, geysers, and beutiful volcanoes. There are also a few museums for you to attend. Some of these places are:

  • La Fortuna Waterfall, a beutiful waterfall that is located in La Fortuna, a city in Norther Costa Rica. As you hike up the side of the waterfall you get to experience the beuties of La Fortuna.
  • Hanging Bridges in La Fortuna, another location in La Fortuna. Here you get to hike over bridges made of string and wood as you get a great overlook of the forest underneath. This is a great place for photographers to travel and snap some great pictures of the wildlife.
  • Precolumbian Gold Museum, a museum located in San José, it contains a bunch of shiny artifacts from the era before the Columbians.
