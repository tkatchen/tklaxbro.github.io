Bienvenido a Costa Rica!

  Costa Rica is a country located in Central America and is known for its great coffee and chocolate. It is one of the most popular retirement places in all of Central America, with beutiful lakes, quiet rural areas, and a booming marketplace it is a magnet for retired people. Some positives about Costa Rica include:

  • Great, tropical, climate being a constant 70-80 degree throughout the year making it perfect for beaches.
  • A great community, everybody goes outside and have a great social life with eachother
  • A lot of cheap, worth things. Some of these include cheap housing, where a house of the same size can cost 2 to 3 times cheaper than in America. They also have a cheap, reliable health care system where you can pay $130 for private healthcare or $30-50 for public health care.
  • They have a very solid and stable democracy. There is a very solid Constitution and with elected officials on a term there is a very low chance of an overthrow occuring.

Interesting Facts!

  • Costa Rica was one of the first countries to completely legalize all drugs, in 1988 they decriminalized all drugs, this failed and created multiple homeless drug addicts.
  • Average pay for a laborer is $10(5700 Colón)/day, the highest in central America
  • 1 USD = 570.03 Costa Rican Coloón
  • 801 Miles of Coastline
  • 750,000 species of insects live there
