La musica de Costa Rica!

  Costa Rican music varies between the adult and the children. The adults prefer to listen to music that is much more dance-oriented. This include the Soca, Cumbia, Merengue, and Salsa. The kids prefer to listen to hip music, two artists that are popular in Costa Rica are Manuel Turizo and and J Balvin. The First song is Una Lady Como tú. This song is by Manuel Turizo and come out in March of 2017. It has achieved 500 million vers on Youtube.

  The second song is Mi Gente by J Balvin. This was released in Jun of 2017 and has reached over 1 billion views on Youtube.
